# Knowledge/research/

> Research outputs and synthesis. Things you've investigated and distilled.
> Raw notes don't belong here — only processed, usable knowledge.

---

## What Goes Here

- Research summaries from user interviews, competitor analysis, or market exploration
- Synthesis documents that distill multiple sources into insights
- Sub-agent research outputs (when you say "spin up" and delegate a research task)
- Data analysis results

**What doesn't go here:** Raw transcripts, unprocessed notes (those go in `_temp/`), or articles you want to read someday (those go in `Knowledge/reference/`).

---

## File Format

Filename: `[topic]-research.md` or `[YYYY-MM]-[topic]-synthesis.md`

Use `Templates/research-summary.md` as your starting point.

Key sections:
- **Executive Summary** — 3–5 sentences: what did you learn, what should you do?
- **Key Findings** — numbered, each with evidence and confidence level
- **Recommendations** — immediate actions, future considerations, what NOT to do
- **Open Questions** — what you still don't know

---

## Connecting Research to Work

After creating a research file:
1. Link it from the relevant `Projects/[name]/prd.md`
2. If it produces a decision → log in `Memory/decisions/`
3. If it reveals a recurring pattern → log in `Memory/patterns/`
4. Add a task to `Tasks/active.md` for the recommended action
