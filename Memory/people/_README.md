# Memory/people/

> Stakeholder context. What you know about the people you work with.
> The goal: never walk into a conversation without context, never draft a message blind.

---

## When to Write Here

Create or update a person's file:
- After any meaningful 1:1 or working session
- When you learn something important about their priorities, style, or concerns
- Before you're about to ask them for something significant
- When a relationship shifts (new role, tension, deepened trust)

---

## File Format

Filename: `[firstname-lastname].md` (e.g., `sarah-chen.md`, `marcus-wells.md`)

```markdown
# [Name]

**Role:** [Their title and org]
**Relationship:** [Manager / Direct report / XFN partner / Stakeholder / Mentor / etc.]
**Last updated:** YYYY-MM-DD

## What They Care About

[Their priorities, goals, pressures. What do they optimize for?]

## How They Communicate

[Preferred style: async/sync? Detail-heavy or summary-first? Direct or indirect?]

## What Works With Them

[Approaches, formats, or framings that land well]

## What Doesn't Work

[Things that create friction or misunderstanding]

## History & Context

[Key moments, decisions, or interactions that shaped this relationship]

## Open Loops

[Things you've said you'll do, follow-ups pending, or questions you want to ask]

## Recent Interactions

**YYYY-MM-DD:** [Brief summary of what was discussed or decided]
**YYYY-MM-DD:** [...]
```

---

## Example Entry (abbreviated)

```markdown
# Jordan Rivera

**Role:** VP of Product, [Company]
**Relationship:** Skip-level manager
**Last updated:** 2026-04-15

## What They Care About

Revenue impact first. Moves fast. Hates being surprised.
Very focused on Q2 growth targets — everything gets evaluated through that lens.

## How They Communicate

Prefers Slack over email. Short messages with a clear ask.
If you're sharing an update, lead with the number, then the story.

## What Works

Showing up with data and a recommendation — not just a problem.
Being direct about trade-offs.

## What Doesn't Work

Long context-setting before getting to the point.
Asking for guidance before you've tried a first answer.

## Open Loops

- [ ] Follow up on the pricing discussion from April 12th sync
- [ ] Share revised rollout timeline by end of month
```
